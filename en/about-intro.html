Company Profile

Founded in 2005, HB International Cargo Transportation Co., Ltd. is located in the Pudong International Airport Comprehensive Bonded Area of China (Shanghai) Pilot Free Trade Zone. The Company has contracted an express warehouse and a bonded warehouse and operates as a comprehensive logistics service provider ranging from international trade, air transport, marine transport, express delivery, customs clearance and warehousing. The Company not only has the Grade I qualification as of a cargo transportation agent, but also acts as one of the licensed dealers of China civil aviation international shipping services. Moreover, approved by the Customs House as a Category A customs broker, the Company holds the professional import/export declaration qualifications that are registered in and approved by the Customs House. At present, the Company is applying for the formalities as of a Category AA customs broker, which is deemed a solid step forward for the Company to become a standardized and international service provider.

By virtue of the well-developed domestic and international freight forwarder networks, the Company delivers the services covering the customs declaration and transportation of the imported and exported international cargos. With the right to engage in import and export trade, the Company provides customers with the value-added services covering, among other things, the import/export trade agency and works out the sound and integrated solutions of foreign trade agency and international logistics.

The Company adheres to such core management concept as to be a “Trustworthy, Professional and Efficient” service provider and works with such business philosophy as to “Keep Making Innovative and Sustainable Development”. In order to render more professional, meticulous and caring services to customers, we’ve built up a sophisticated logistics information service platform and realized a seamless linkage of various segments of the logistics supply chain as well as the real-time information inquiries, which help customers to save their logistic costs. Thanks to the outstanding professional quality, expertise and efficient teamwork and well-developed service systems, we take the initiatives to create the favorable win-win situations together with our customers.
