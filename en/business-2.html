International Express Delivery

Commercial Express Delivery

Deal with sales, warehousing, sorting, distribution and collection of imported/exported goods.

The customs declarations of imported/exported goods shall be made in the following four categories:

Category A: Document Declaration

Category B: Duty-Free Specimens & Advertising Pieces

Category C: Dutiable Specimens & Advertising Pieces

Category D: Trade-Type Goods

Import of Private Goods

Acting as a sales agent for and on behalf of individual persons to handle the customs clearance of private goods, which are restricted by the Customs House to be less than RMB 1,000 yuan in value per piece and the Company shall make declaration to the Customs for release through inspections by presenting the consignee’s ID Card.

Cross-Border E-Business

The Company handles the collection, distribution, space booking and customs declaration of the goods imported/exported through cross-border e-business. The customs declarations for imports through cross-border e-business are processed subject to the pre-categorization and paperless customs clearance. Collective customs declarations are made for the deliveries in batches, as the documentations, including the e-contracts, e-invoices and packing lists shall be sent to the Customs House. In case of exports through cross-border e-business, the Company handles by way of declaration-by-deal following the approved release of the export shipping document data as declared. The door-to-door logistics services are available by collecting goods at customers’ premises and making distributions upon release by the Customs. Meanwhile, the quarantine inspection authorities are invited by the Company to stay working in our warehousing facilities for surveillance purpose, which ensures swift declarations and inspections that are processed any time without delay.
