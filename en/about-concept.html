Logo Interpretation

The Logo is designed with such elements of two English letters “b” and ”h”, while integrating the images of an airplane and sky. The characters on the logo are designed to be steady as well as generous that imply the safe and reliable nature of the industry, with the airplane to protrude a sense of speed. The whole logo conveys a dynamic sense producing a fast and efficient visual impression, while acting together with the steady characters to create an effect of pursuing speed in steadiness and creating impetus in serenity. A golden arc appears like a flying orbit characteristic of air transport, yielding a perfectly natural impression of melting coexistence.

　　

In terms of color match, the pattern has a red background, which represents passion and prospect. An arrow points at the red color, which indicates a wonderful development prospect for the Company. The golden characters are used to demonstrate the Company’s sincerity as well as highly efficient service attitude.

　　

It is expected to convey the company’s exploring concept characteristic of constant pursuit of excellent performance.
